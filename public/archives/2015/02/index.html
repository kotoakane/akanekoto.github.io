Profile

I'm a Ph.D. student in the department of Information and Computer Science at Keio University. I'm part of System Software Laboratory (sslab) where I'm advised by Kenji Kono. I'm interested in operating systems, cloud computing, and virtualization. My current research focuses on design and implementation of efficient live virtual machine migration in clouds.
